Artist Statement

When it comes to my artwork and how I express it, it all depends on the medium, how I feel that day, and the things that motivate and inspire me. I like to make things that either have deep feelings intertwined with it, some regular fan art just for the fun of it and many others. I don’t want my art to be tied down into one specific genre and type, I enjoy expressing myself in different forms of art. read more
